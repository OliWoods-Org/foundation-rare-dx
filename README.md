> **95% of rare diseases have no FDA-approved treatment.** The diagnostic odyssey destroys families financially and emotionally. AI can match symptoms to conditions in seconds instead of years.

## The Solution

Symptom-to-diagnosis matching, genomic variant interpretation, clinical trial finder, patient community connector, medical passport generation.

## Agents

**SymptomMatcher** — Cross-reference phenotypes against OMIM, Orphanet, medical literature
**GenomicInterpreter** — WES/WGS variant interpretation assistant
**TrialFinder** — Clinical trial matching specific to rare diseases
**CommunityConnector** — Link newly diagnosed with condition-specific support groups
**MedicalPassport** — Generate summary document for new providers

## Safety

MANDATORY disclaimer: This is a decision-support tool, not a diagnostic device. Always consult qualified medical professionals.

## Data Sources

OMIM, Orphanet, HPO, ClinVar, ClinicalTrials.gov, GARD, PubMed, gnomAD
